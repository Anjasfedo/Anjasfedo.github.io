## Overview

A powerful analytics platform designed specifically for e-commerce businesses. Track sales, monitor inventory levels, analyze customer behavior, and optimize your marketing efforts.

## Key Features

- **Real-time Dashboard**: Live updates on sales and visitor activity
- **Sales Analytics**: Detailed breakdown of revenue, orders, and conversion rates
- **Inventory Tracking**: Monitor stock levels and get low-stock alerts
- **Customer Insights**: Understand your customers with behavioral analytics
- **Integration Ready**: Connect with Shopify, WooCommerce, and custom platforms

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: Next.js 14, React, TypeScript
- **Backend**: Node.js, Express, PostgreSQL
- **Caching**: Redis for real-time data
- **Visualization**: Custom chart components with D3.js
- **Payments**: Stripe integration for revenue tracking
